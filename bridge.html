BRIDGE MUSEUM

Bridge Museum is an innovative museum that bridges the gap between reality and the virtual world, expanding the entire city of New York into its exhibition space through VR technology. Visitors can explore various locations throughout the city, scanning codes to experience the exhibits, interact with them, and watch animations. These thoughtfully redesigned exhibits are showcased at specific sites, telling unique, regionally-focused stories that break the traditional boundaries and definitions of a museum. This approach provides visitors with new inspiration, encouraging them to delve deeper into the context of the exhibits and igniting their passion for telling new stories. The museum's design and brand identity break away from convention, employing diverse methods of connection that not only strengthen the relationship between the exhibits and the visitors but also enhance the emotional bond between the visitors and the specific locales. This presents a fresh narrative perspective where everyone becomes a unique New Yorker. Bridge Museum is more than just a platform for display—it’s a stage for co-creating stories between the audiences and the city.

The museum's collection function further deepens the visitor experience. Every moment of interaction between visitors and exhibits is recorded in real-time, creating a unique personal "memory ticket." These tickets can be saved after the visit and viewed in 3D using Apple Vision Pro, allowing visitors to relive the interactive scenes. This approach not only reinforces the initial points of memory but also enhances the emotional connection with the exhibits, making each visit a profound and unforgettable journey.This version aims to clearly convey the museum's innovation while emphasizing its integration with modern technology and the enhancement of emotional experiences.
