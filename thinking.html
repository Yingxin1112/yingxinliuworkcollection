THINKING/MAKING

This is my collection of papers. One of the charms of a book lies in the process of opening it. The act of flipping through, how the book is opened, and the effort required to obtain the key content are also important aspects of a book. I have arranged the content in order of importance from highest to lowest (index, title, abstract, key points, main text, and notes), distributing these elements throughout different locations in the book (from outside to inside) to align with the process of opening a book. Readers can choose how deeply to open the book based on their interests and priorities to access the content they need.
